Into the present

We all live in a world of progression. But have you ever thought about the world around us? the ecosystem we live in? we have only learnt about it in our textbooks.....but have we ever payed any attention to it..... Many might agree with me about not giving time to ourselves amid our busy schedule . Let's have a quick Tour to the way I live...... The things in my world......

Green

Green is a cool color...is it not? Well I find green so calming that I tend to stay around it a lot. I am surrounded by plants always...... Greening is one of my hobbies....Gardening is very soothing.......I usually lose the track of time while leafing around.

Feel

I feel the things people see. I believe that everything around us expresses itself in an unique way. one of my other hobby is Dancing. The ideology of dancing, what I believe, is communicating.  

Imagining 


One way of spending time when you are bored is to imagine things. Practically, this can be visualised as meditating...... Imagining helps ME to calm down, and it keeps me collected too. Imagining can also be accompanied by the habit of reading, for example. I read a variety of fiction.

Living in the present.


This might be one of the oldest things that humans have been doing. Keep a record of the things that I have been doing or going through. This personally helps me to pick up the positive things that have been going on with me and around me.
